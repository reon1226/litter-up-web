# Project Vision

> Transform litter into art, and transform people through the experience.

---

# Litter Up Vision

Litter Up is not just an image processing application.

It is a platform designed to encourage people to participate in environmental action through creativity, enjoyment, and community.

Instead of treating litter collection as an obligation, Litter Up transforms it into a creative experience where every collected piece contributes to a larger work of art.

The goal is to make environmental action something people genuinely want to do.

---

# Mission

Create a world where cleaning the environment becomes a fun and meaningful activity.

Litter Up aims to inspire people to take action voluntarily by combining environmental conservation with creativity.

Rather than relying on rewards or pressure, we believe people are more likely to continue participating when the experience itself is enjoyable.

---

# Vision

Build a future where every piece of litter collected contributes to something beautiful.

Through collaborative digital artwork created from photographs of collected litter, we hope to demonstrate that individual actions can create significant collective impact.

---

# Core Values

## 1. Action before awareness

Real change begins with action.

We design experiences that naturally encourage people to participate instead of simply educating them.

---

## 2. Creativity drives participation

People participate more when activities are enjoyable.

Creativity is not decoration.

It is the engine that motivates action.

---

## 3. Every contribution matters

Every photograph.

Every participant.

Every piece of litter.

All contributions become part of a larger artwork.

---

## 4. Simplicity

The product should be understandable by anyone within a few minutes.

Every feature must reduce complexity instead of adding it.

---

## 5. Accessibility

Litter Up should be usable regardless of age, language, or technical knowledge.

The experience should be intuitive.

---

# Problem Statement

Many people understand environmental issues.

However, understanding does not always lead to action.

Traditional cleanup events often struggle with long-term engagement because the experience itself is not sufficiently motivating.

Litter Up addresses this gap by transforming environmental action into a rewarding creative experience.

---

# Solution

Users collect litter.

Users photograph the litter.

The photographs become building blocks of a collaborative artwork.

The completed artwork represents everyone's contribution.

The artwork itself becomes proof that environmental action can create beauty.

---

# Long-Term Goal

Become the world's leading platform that combines environmental conservation, public art, and community participation.

Litter Up aims to collaborate with schools, municipalities, companies, NGOs, and communities around the world.

---

# Product Philosophy

Technology exists to support human behavior.

Artificial intelligence is not the product.

Image processing is not the product.

The real product is the experience that motivates people to take positive action.

Every design decision should strengthen this experience.

---

# Success Criteria

The project is successful when people say:

"I joined because it looked fun."

"I want to participate again."

"I invited my friends."

"I never thought cleaning could feel like creating art."

These reactions matter more than downloads, page views, or technical achievements.

---

# Development Principle

Every new feature must answer one question:

"Does this make people more excited to participate?"

If the answer is no,

the feature should not be developed.

---

# Motto

Cleaning creates art.

Art inspires people.

People change the world.
